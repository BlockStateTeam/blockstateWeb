Project: Art Enthusiasm Version 2

The "Art Enthusiasm" Add-On has taken the chart by storm, becoming the most downloaded and beloved Add-On out of all BlockState collection. Your enthusiasm for this remarkable Add-On has not gone unnoticed, and it's time to announce that the long-awaited update is finally here - introducing Art Enthusiasm Version 2!

Now, you must be eager to learn about the exciting new tools in Art Enthusiasm Version 2. If you need a refresher on how to use the existing features, please refer to the first version instruction!. Now, let's dive straight into the thrilling additions that await you:
Introducing an exciting array of fresh tools and a vibrant spectrum of new colors has just become available!
"Tool preview" is also added! You can see what tool the cursor is hovering on!
Let's see how to use the new tools!
The fill tool works just like the reset tool, but instead fill the whole canvas with White color, it will fill whatever color is being selected by the brush!
Please note that there's a big change on Render system! In the previous version, whenever you draw anything, the system will auto-render it out and you can see the drawing on your map immediately.
Unfortunately, auto-rendering is very computational expensive! So, in this version 2, auto-rendering is disable. You will need to do manually rendering!
If you forget how to do manually render, just click this render button!
Ta-da! Now your beautiful art is now viewable on the map!
Also, a new reminder is added for people who keep forgetting to sneak to exit!
