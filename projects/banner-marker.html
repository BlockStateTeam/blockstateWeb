Project: Banner Marker

Are you tired of feeling jealous when you see someone marking locations on a map with banners in Minecraft Java Edition? Well, your wait is over because this Add-On is here to bring that feature to Minecraft Bedrock!
Let's dive into how this Add-On works! It's simple; all you need is a banner and a map - that's it! Let's say you want to mark your beautiful river port on the map.
Simply hold the map and then right-click on the banner!
Ta-da! Now you'll know exactly where your lovely port is located!
Note that if you want to remove the marker, SNEAK! And right-click the banner with the map in your hand!
That's everything! We hope you all enjoy it!
