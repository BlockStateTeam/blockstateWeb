Project: Working TV

Watching videos in Minecraft? It might sound unbelievable, but now, thanks to this Add-On, it's a reality! Introducing Working TV, an Add-On that enables you to seamlessly play videos in Minecraft. Choose any video you desireâ€”all within the realm of vanilla Minecraft! No need for mods, plugins, or external software; it's the essence of pure vanilla Minecraft!
This is the amazing TV! A brand new shiny block that allows you to play video in Minecraft! It's also fully customizable, allowing you to upload your own videos to play in Minecraft!
Let's start with its crafting recipe. It's pretty simple, just 5 iron ingots, 1 copper ingot and 1 redstone dust! Here's how it looks like in the crafting table!
To turn on the TV, simply right-click the TV. You can also turn it off by right-clicking it again! Now, the TV can play up to 5 videos, to change the video, simply right-click the copper antenna on top of the TV.

CUSTOMIZATION SECTION

Upload a maximum of 5 videos to include them in the TV! Videos need to be:
> In .mp4 or .webm format
> In horizontal orientation
> Less than 4-ish minutes long per video (Due to Minecraft's limitation)
> You need to wait until the preview video is playable before you can download the Addon!
